AMD Investment Thesis

AMD 的投资逻辑正在从 CPU share gainer + PC cycle recovery，转向 AI accelerator + Data Center platform company。

Ticker: AMD View: Constructive but valuation-sensitive Key Driver: AI GPU + Data Center 核心敏感性：Revenue CAGR vs Terminal EBIT Margin
Thesis Financials Cases Valuation Catalysts

1. Thesis Summary

核心观点：AMD 是一个高上行、高预期的 AI infrastructure equity。股票能否继续表现，取决于 AI GPU / Data Center 收入能否足够快地放大，并把毛利率和 EBIT margin 拉到足以支撑当前估值的水平。

Data Center 成为主引擎

FY2025 Data Center revenue 达到 $16.6B，同比增长 32%；Q1 2026 Data Center revenue 达到 $5.8B，同比增长 57%。

AI GPU 变成实质贡献

MI350 / MI450 / Helios rack-scale systems 和 Meta 6GW 合作提升了 AI 需求可见度。

利润率仍有上行空间

FY2025 gross margin 为 50%，Q2 2026 non-GAAP gross margin guidance 约 56%。

3. Bull / Base / Bear Cases

Bull Case

AI GPU + EPYC 重新定价 AMD

MI350 / MI450 / Helios ramp 超预期，Meta 6GW 合作带来长期订单可见度，hyperscalers 把 AMD 作为 NVIDIA 之外的第二供应商。

  • Revenue CAGR: 15%–20%
  • Gross Margin: 55%–58%
  • Terminal EBIT Margin: 30%–34%
  • WACC: 8.0%–9.0%
  • Terminal Growth: 3.0%–3.5%
Base Case

增长明确，但估值依赖利润率扩张

AMD 在 AI GPU 和 Data Center 中取得可观份额，但仍明显落后于 NVIDIA；估值是否成立取决于 gross margin 和 operating leverage。

  • Revenue CAGR: 10%–14%
  • Gross Margin: 52%–55%
  • R&D / Revenue: 21%–23%
  • SG&A / Revenue: 8%–9%
  • Terminal EBIT Margin: 24%–28%
  • WACC: 9.0%–10.0%
  • Terminal Growth: 2.5%–3.0%
Bear Case

AI 叙事兑现慢于市场预期

客户仍高度依赖 NVIDIA，AMD software ecosystem 成为 adoption bottleneck，MI450 / Helios ramp 推迟或毛利率低于预期。

  • Revenue CAGR: 6%–9%
  • Gross Margin: 49%–51%
  • Terminal EBIT Margin: 18%–22%
  • WACC: 10.5%–11.5%
  • Terminal Growth: 2.0%

4. Valuation Debate

MetricAMDPeer MedianInterpretation
EV / Sales ~14.0x ~14.7x 接近 peer median，收入倍数层面 AMD 不明显贵。
EV / EBITDA ~72.1x ~39.5x 显著高于 peers，市场提前定价未来 AI / Data Center 利润率扩张。
估值争论的核心：市场并不是按当前 EBITDA 给 AMD 定价，而是在提前定价未来 AI GPU 和 Data Center 的规模化利润率。
Key valuation questions: 1. 未来 3–5 年 Data Center revenue 是否能持续超预期？ 2. AI GPU gross margin 是否足够高？ 3. Terminal EBIT margin 能否达到 mid/high-20s 或更高？ 4. 当前股价隐含的 Revenue CAGR 和 EBIT margin 是否过高？

7. What Would Change the View

More Positive

  • Data Center revenue accelerates beyond expectations.
  • MI450 / Helios wins multiple hyperscaler customers beyond Meta.
  • Gross margin sustains mid-50s or higher.
  • Management raises long-term AI GPU revenue outlook.
  • Evidence AMD is becoming a durable second-source AI platform.

More Negative

  • AI GPU ramp is delayed.
  • Gross margin guidance weakens despite Data Center growth.
  • Meta partnership appears low-margin or back-end loaded.
  • NVIDIA widens performance/software ecosystem lead.
  • Data Center CPU share gains slow materially.

8. Bottom Line

AMD works if: AI GPU revenue scales + Data Center mix rises + gross margin moves into mid-50s + EBIT margin moves toward high-20s / low-30s AMD struggles if: AI revenue is slower or margin expansion disappoints or market stops paying premium multiples for AI semiconductor growth

For modeling, the most important sensitivity table is Revenue CAGR vs Terminal EBIT Margin because that is where the AMD thesis will ultimately be proven or disproven.
