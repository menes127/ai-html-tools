4. Beat / Miss Explanation

Why Revenue Beat

  1. Data Center demand was stronger than expected.
  2. EPYC server CPUs continued to perform well.
  3. Instinct GPU shipments ramped.
  4. Client remained healthy.
  5. Embedded stabilized.

Why EPS Beat

Revenue upside + favorable mix + gross margin expansion + operating leverage = EPS beat

5. AI GPU / Data Center Commentary

AMD highlighted strengthening demand for MI350, MI450 Series, Helios rack-scale AI systems, and AMD Instinct GPU shipments.

Why this matters: The bull case for AMD requires AMD to become a durable second-source AI platform alongside NVIDIA. This quarter does not prove that fully, but it strengthens the argument that AMD is gaining relevance in large-scale AI infrastructure.
EPYC server CPU share gains + Instinct GPU ramp + MI450 / Helios adoption + hyperscaler AI infrastructure demand
